Mud Pie

Ingredients:

  • Orange peels
  • Egg shells
  • Water
  • Dirt
  • Crushed leaves

Directions:

  1. Gather ingredients; dirt, bark, rocks, pebbles, etc...
  2. Add water until consistency of bread.
  3. Let sit for 2-4 hours in afternoon sun.
